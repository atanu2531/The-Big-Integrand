# Senior Data Scientist

## Role Overview
We are seeking a Senior Data Scientist to lead our machine learning initiatives. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and deploying advanced analytical models to solve complex business problems.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable machine learning models and data pipelines.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities for leveraging data.
- Conduct advanced statistical analysis and communicate insights to stakeholders.
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to the team's technical growth.
- Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in AI and machine learning.

## Requirements
- Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
- 5+ years of experience in data science or machine learning roles.
- Proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks, specifically **TensorFlow**.
- Strong experience in orchestrating data workflows and ML pipelines using **Apache Airflow**.
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerization (Docker).
- Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms and statistical modeling.
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
